read our story

we've been serving delicious indian & pakistani halal food since 2010

Our General Manager Mrs. Bhavana Chirag Solanki opened doors of Shahi Rasoi with an simple idea of bringing original Indian & Pakistani taste to Canada. Her passion of cooking healthy and quality food has been knitted into culture at Shahi Rasoi.

Mr. Chirag Parmar & Mrs. Bhavna Chirag Solanki co-founded Shahi Rasoi in March 2010 with only 10 item. Along with time our menu grew as well and today we are serving almost 40 item. Currently, we accept catering orders up to 50 to 300 people.
